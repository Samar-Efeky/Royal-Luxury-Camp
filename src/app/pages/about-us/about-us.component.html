About Royal Luxury Camp

Welcome our dear guests and Travelers of the world to an unforgettable Authentic experience of the Moroccan Sahara Desert while always staying in Luxury & Comfort. We help our camp guests to focus on exploring the golden part of our mother Nature. The Sahara.

So to do that we are offering you all our glamping sites at your disposal, fully equipped with all what you need to stay comfortable and enjoy the staying and the discovery.

Beside our luxury camping and glamping sites we offer travel tours and trips to assist you in visiting the top famous spots and places worth visiting in Morocco. Then to even give you the best service and experience we help you adventure the Sahara through exciting desert activities where you will enjoy everything the Sahara desert has to give to its venturers
